I designed and built a website for spontaneous art discovery, to make fine art more accessible.

I love art: I even briefly went to art school. But I've noticed many of my friends feel uncomfortable in art museums. When I've asked them about it, it's because they don't know how to tell if something is "good", or how to talk about it.

I don't think you need a rich background in art history to appreciate art, so I designed and built a website to walk people through a wide variety of famous (and lesser known) works, giving them just enough information to talk about art with confidence!
