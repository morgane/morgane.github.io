I worked with SF Iron, a local weightlifting gym, to develop their brand, and design a website and logo for them.

They're a new gym that wanted to appeal to serious athletes and beginners alike, especially people who traditionally avoid weightlifting. In other words, they didn't want to be yet another gym full of really big dudes. I'm happy to say that after our work together, they're still doing really well and have a diverse clientele.
