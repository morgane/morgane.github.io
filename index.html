I worked with SF Iron, a local weightlifting gym, to develop their brand, and design a website and logo for them.

I used extensive user testing, carefully edited copy, and a strong visual strategy to design for their mission: Strength for All. They wanted to appeal to serious athletes and beginners alike, especially people who traditionally avoid weightlifting. In other words, they didn't want to be yet another gym full of really big dudes. I'm happy to say they're still doing really well, and have a diverse clientele.

I've written in-depth about my process on this project, and of course, you can see samples of the work on Behance.
