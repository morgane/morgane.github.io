FAQ

Q: How much are tickets?

A: Free!

Q: What if I got tickets but I can't go?

A: Just cancel your order on Eventbrite so someone on the waitlist can go instead :)

Q: Is this only for designers?

A: Nope. While some topics will be more relevant to designers, anyone is welcome to join.

Q: Will there be food?

A: No, so bring snacks!

Q: Is this a safe space?

A: YES. We want everyone to feel comfortable. No harrassment or abusive behavior will be tolerated, period. If anyone's ruining the night for you, let a moderator know. We'll take care of it.
